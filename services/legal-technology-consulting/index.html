Cloud Services for Law Firms & Paperless Law Office

If your office is not already using cloud services, now is the time to jump on the rocket ship. Cloud products allow cross platform document and data management. This allows instant access to important documents on the fly while in court, away from your desk, or traveling.

Cloud Services

Streamline workflow and optimize office production by utilizing cloud software and services. Cloud services enable you to conduct business in a virtual manner while away from your office. Our vendors provide hardware and the backbone cloud software while we provide the optimization for lawyers, technical support, setup, file migration, and ongoing cloud management. Moving some, or all, of your office to the cloud will ensure that you and your team are always operating at 100% capacity.

Cloud Service Products Include

  • Secure Document Storage

    • Secure Encryption for Sensitive Files
    • Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) & AES-256 bit encryption
    • No Expensive Equipment to Maintain
  • Practice Management
    • Matter & Case Management
    • Time Tracking
    • Online Client Billing & Invoicing
    • Matter & Case Management
  • Virtual Office
    • Custom Email Solutions
    • Calendar & Scheduling
    • Voicemail Transcription
    • Project Management
    • Enterprise Instant Messaging

Private Cloud Solutions

Private cloud storage solutions are ideal for medium to large firms with heightened security concerns. All data, client files, and other sensitive material are stored on site with the law firm. Private cloud services offer all the benefits of the standard cloud, with the added benefit of never having to entrust files with a 3rd party. Each private cloud is setup with complete control in the hands of the law firm

Private Cloud Solutions Include

  • Private Cloud Hardware
  • Complete Control Over Documents
  • No 3rd Party Intermediaries
  • 2-way Secure Encryption
  • AES-256 bit & XTS-AES 128 bit encryption
